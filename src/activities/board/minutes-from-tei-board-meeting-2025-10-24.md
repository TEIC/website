## TEI Board Meeting: 24 October 2025

Attending: Diane Jakacki, James Cummings, Magdalena Turska, Elisa Beshero-Bondar, Gimena del Rio Riande, Hugh Cayless, Dimitra Grigoriou 
Apologies:  Constance Crompton



### Agenda Items


*   Conference Updates
    * TEI2025: 
        * Board thanks Jagiellonian University and all those involved in the organisation, production, and delivering of it for a wonderful conference
     * TEI2026: 
        * Waiting on website
        * SSHRC Connections grant proposal in progress



* Council Report 
     * Council wants to keep nominated individuals who are not elected for a TEI-C Technical Contributors Program. 
        * This would consist of an education program of tutorials, github, commandline, building the TEI-C Guidelines locally, submitting pull requests, advanced ODD structures, etc.  
        * Requests board to keep pressure on Council via Council reports to Board
        * Dimitra Grigoriou  recommends the #DariahTeach tutorials that Council might be able to direct people to in order to not reinvent the wheel.


* Membership Drive
    * Board is keen to preserve the Council face-to-face meetings: This builds rapport and rewards and increases their dedication to council works. Council members donate a substantial amount of in-kind time.
    * AGM: members asked the Board to be creative, seek now revenue streams
    * Option to donate towards others' memberships 
    * Explore grant-funding for development of TEI P6
    * #WeAreTheTEI to highlight successes 
    * Need a mechanism to make the social media coordinator position bigger, more involved
    * We need multilingual social media ambassadors
